TheShovel.rocks Discord Server Archive

Updated April 22, 2025

Why does this exist?

This was made to archive the content of the TheShovel.rocks Discord server. It is a public archive of the server's content, including messages, files, and other data. The archive is intended to be a permanent record of the server's history and to provide a way for users to access the server's content even after the server has been deleted.

Why was the server deleted?

This server meant a lot to me and my friends. It's where I have shared most of the stuff I made. Recently, bad people started to join. People who don't like me. What once was a safe heaven for me to showcase my work and to get feedback on my projects now became a place where I felt unwelcome. People didn't join for my work, they joined for me, and that was not my goal. I hope that by deleting the server, I can move on and focus on my work. My mental health was never the best, and this did not help to improve it, or at least to help me cope with it, it made it worse.
